## 🌟 Core Features

### 🧠 Gameplay Mechanics
- **Circular Letter Wheel:** Innovative touch/mouse selection for a tactile feel.
- **50 Scaling Levels:** Progress from simple 3-letter words to complex 7+ letter challenges.
- **Dynamic Grid:** The puzzle board automatically adapts to level complexity.
- **Shuffle System:** Re-arrange letters instantly to find new perspectives.

### 💎 Economy & Rewards
- **Persistent Points (Gems):** Earn 50 Gems for every level completed.
- **Daily Rewards:** Claim **100 Free Gems** every 24 hours.
- **Power-up Shop:**
  - 💡 **Hint (100 Gems):** Reveal a random letter in the grid.
  - 🔍 **Super Hint (300 Gems):** Instantly reveal an entire hidden word.
- **LocalStorage Persistence:** All your progress and gems are saved automatically.

### 🎨 Visuals & Audio
- **Pixel-Art Aesthetic:** Curated professional backgrounds for every single level.
- **Glassmorphism UI:** Modern frosted-glass panels and high-tech typography (Orbitron).
- **Immersive Audio:** Relaxing gameplay music with an easy-to-use Mute toggle.
- **Smooth Animations:** Buttery-smooth transitions and celebrate popups.

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Play Locally
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Rokibul-Islam-Robi/PuzzleBit.git
   ```
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. No installation or build steps required!

### Deployment
Optimized for **Vercel**. Just connect your GitHub repo and it will deploy instantly as a high-performance static site.

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Observe the Grid:** Look at the hidden word rows at the top.
2. **Select Letters:** Click and drag (or click sequentially) on the bottom wheel to form words.
3. **Release to Submit:** The game automatically checks if your word is correct.
4. **Use Power-ups:** If stuck, use Gems to reveal letters or shuffle the wheel.
5. **Complete & Earn:** Find all words to finish the level and claim your 50 Gems!
